Quick Help Topics

Before sending an email, check if your question is covered in these common topics:

Supported File Types

We support JPEG, PNG, WebP, and HEIC files up to 10MB each. Most common image formats work perfectly.

Compression Quality

Use 70-80% quality for web images, 85-95% for print. Lower values = smaller files but reduced quality.

Privacy & Security

Your images are automatically deleted within 24 hours. We don't store, analyze, or share your photos.

Mobile Usage

Our tool works great on phones and tablets. No app needed - just use your mobile browser.

Download Issues

Can't download your compressed images? Try refreshing the page or using a different browser.

Batch Processing

Upload up to 20 images at once. All will be compressed with your selected quality settings.
